Razor Shogun

Warrior-inspired emblem blending East and West

Category
Logo Design
Style
Illustration · Emblem
Tools
Adobe Illustrator
Year
2024

The Concept

Razor Shogun is built around one idea: controlled destruction. The kind of force that is precise, disciplined, and lethal. Every line in this logo had to feel like it was drawn with intent — nothing wasted, nothing soft.

The brief called for something that could live in the streetwear and gaming space simultaneously — carrying the weight of Japanese warrior culture while speaking the language of modern design.

The Execution

The illustration work centres on a skull in samurai armour — a classic symbol of warrior honour fused with fearlessness. The skull removes the human, leaving only the spirit of the fighter.

Custom illustration was drawn in Adobe Illustrator using precision anchor-point control. Every stroke was deliberate — the armour detailing, the blade angles, the typography beneath. The result feels handcrafted yet scalable, equally powerful at pocket size or poster size.

The bold wordmark beneath anchors the illustration — clean, uppercase, uncompromising.
