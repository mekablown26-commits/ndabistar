Pool Tournament

High-energy event poster built to stop the scroll and fill the room

Category
Poster Design
Format
Print · Digital
Tools
Illustrator · Photoshop
Year
2024

The Assignment

A pre-tournament pool game needed a poster that would build hype before the event even started. That means one look, maximum impact — the viewer has to feel the energy before they read a single word.

The design needed to work across formats — A3 print, WhatsApp stories, social media tiles — without losing any of its punch.

The Design Decisions

The headline typography is the hero — massive, distressed, layered. "PRE TOURNAMENT POOL GAME" hits like a statement, not an announcement. The distressing adds grit and texture, suggesting competition and stakes.

The colour palette is warm and high-contrast — gold on dark, echoing the feeling of stadium lights and trophies. Every element is arranged to create visual hierarchy that guides the eye from the headline to the details to the call to action.

The result is a poster that doesn't ask for your attention — it commands it.
