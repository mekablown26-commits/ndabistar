Renegade Emblem

Badge design for the bold, the rebellious, the unapologetic

Category
Emblem · Badge Design
Style
Illustration · Typography
Tools
Adobe Illustrator
Year
2024

The Idea

Renegade is a word that means something. It means someone who refuses to follow the rules — who carves their own path regardless of what the crowd does. This emblem needed to embody that spirit completely.

The design brief was open-ended: create a mark that feels like it belongs on a jacket, a helmet, a team kit. Something with presence, history, and edge.

The Craft

The emblem uses a layered shield structure — classic badge architecture that signals legacy and trust, subverted by the Renegade identity beneath it. The typographic treatment is bold and unapologetic.

Every shape was built with vector precision in Illustrator — scalable from a 16px favicon to a 10-foot banner without losing a single detail. The torn paper effect adds tactile energy — like the brand is breaking through the surface it's printed on.

The result is an emblem that feels like it's been around for decades, but was built for right now.
