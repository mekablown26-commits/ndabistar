About Me

I Don't
Create.
I Reveal.

I'm Ian Ndabi Njoroge — they call me Ndabistar. I was drawing before I could explain why. Pencil on paper, walls, screens, code — if it's blank, I see something in it that nobody else does yet.

I design brands that stop people mid-scroll. I build apps that actually work. I animate in 3D. I draw portraits so real they've been presented on stage to powerful people. And I do it all from Nairobi, Kenya — because great work doesn't care where you're from.

I'm not looking for just any opportunity. I'm looking for the one that deserves what I bring. Because whoever gets me doesn't get an intern — they get a creative force that doesn't know how to stop growing.

success is not a destination — it's my possession."
